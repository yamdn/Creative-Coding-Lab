What we know so far about Kindred Spirits?

Kindred spirits are known to be friendly and seemingly encompass the emotions and personalities of our loved ones. Although it has been reported that they appear in dreams, they typically appear when a person needs support to overcome hardships. Also known as "Mnemos Memeris," the name can be translated from the Greek root definitions of memory and poetry.

As you think about the people that you hold close to your heart, the more Kindred Spirits will come to your aid and fill you with determination. Each Kindred Spirit has a unique personality, with some being more outgoing and others being more reserved. The Kindred Spirits are also marked with different colors and a floral pattern.

As you move through the Grassland of Dreams, the Kindred Spirits will follow and orbit around you. The trail they leave lights up the world, and when you hold them close, a warm, fuzzy feeling is emitted. You may be tempted to stay in the field forever, but you must learn to walk on your own eventually and let the Kindred Spirit aid someone else.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature

By thinking about the people you hold close to your heart, click anywhere on the website and a Kindred Spirit will appear. This summoned spirit represents the person you thought of and comes to your aid. Continue to click on the screen and summon as many Kindred Spirits as you need on your journey!

As you move through the Grassland of Dreams using your mouse, the Kindred Spirits follow and orbit around you, making sure you are safe from any danger that may appear. The trail they leave in their movement lights the path you take and makes you feel light enough to continue.

Once you have reached your destination, it's time to let go of the Kindred Spirits and let them roam the Grassland of Dreams again. By moving off the canvas, you let the Kindred Spirits float around on their own as you stand in admiration watching them. Then, by pressing the [SPACEBAR], the Kindred Spirits will disappear and aid someone else on their journey.
